Why Save Water?

Water is essential for all life forms. Saving water helps combat drought, protects our ecosystems, and ensures a better future for generations to come.

Water Saving Tips

  • Turn off the tap while brushing your teeth.
  • Fix leaks promptly.
  • Use a bucket instead of a hose when washing your car.
  • Collect rainwater for watering plants.
  • Install water-saving fixtures.
